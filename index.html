What is Surya AI?

SuryAI is an intelligent solar energy potential monitoring tool. It combines state-of-the-art machine learning, geospatial analysis, and optimization techniques to provide accurate and insightful solar energy estimates for rooftops. Our model has been specially trained for areas with highly dynamic conditions (terrain, vegetation, climate) and as well as sparse populations. SuryAI is the first solar potential mapping tool that has been validated with synthetic rooftops images generated using Stable Diffusion, making it highyl accurate.With SuryAI, you can easily determine how much solar energy can be generated from your rooftop and make informed decisions about solar panel installations. Moreover, we use the latest night light data to estimate an electrification index for your idea, which gives you a sense of how well- connected your local electric grid is, frequencies of loadshedding incidents, etc., thus helping you decide on solar more comprehensively. SuryAI is designed to help homeowners, businesses, and energy planners accurately estimate the solar energy potential of their rooftops using advanced computer vision and spatial analysis. By providing detailed insights into solar irradiance, roof orientation, and optimal solar panel placement (corrected for factors like elevation, air mass, coordinates), SuryAI enables users to maximize green energy production and investment efficiency. Whether you are considering solar energy for personal use or large-scale projects, SuryAI offers a user-friendly, data-driven approach to harnessing the power of the sun. SuryAI integrates machine learning (U-Net, TensorFlow, Keras), spatial analysis (QGIS), and optimization (Particle Swarm Optimization) to accurately estimate SEP. The interactive web-based geospatial portal allows users to analyze SEP and make informed decisions for sustainable energy adoption.
